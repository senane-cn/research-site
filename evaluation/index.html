ICOMOS Evaluation Explorer

Structured exploration of ICOMOS cultural and mixed nomination evaluations.

This project organizes ICOMOS evaluation materials into searchable, comparable datasets. It is designed to support multi-year review of nomination assessment patterns, evidence categories, evaluation conclusions, and protection-management concerns.

Future scope

The Evaluation Explorer will be extended as a multi-year research tool, allowing comparison across ICOMOS evaluation cycles and longer-term review of World Heritage nomination governance.
