Filling the Gaps

Reviewing, updating, and extending the ICOMOS thematic framework since 2004

Filling the Gaps is a medium- to long-term research programme by the ConserVision team. The current phase begins with the ICOMOS 2004 Filling the Gaps review, reconstructing a pre-2005 baseline for typological and thematic analysis.

后续研究将推进 FTG 2026 analysis，并可能形成面向中国和国际世界遗产工作的 action recommendations。
